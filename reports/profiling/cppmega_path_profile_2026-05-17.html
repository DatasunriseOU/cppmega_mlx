IOAccelerator note. macOS reports Metal GPU/compute allocations under IOAccelerator / IOAccelerator (graphics). That is driver accounting, not evidence that we are rendering graphics. There is no useful compile-time switch to disable it for MLX training; disabling that layer would break Metal compute, not make LLM training faster.
